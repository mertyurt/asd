Privacy Policy for YouTube Search Focus

This Chrome extension, YouTube Search Focus, does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from users. The extension operates entirely on your local device to automatically focus the search input on YouTube.

Information Collection and Use

This extension does not collect or share any of your data. It simply enhances your user experience on YouTube by focusing the search bar, and no personal data is accessed, stored, or transmitted in any way.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services or external databases.
